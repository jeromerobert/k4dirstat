A.2. Requirements

In order to successfully use , you need KDE 1.1. Foobar.lib is required in order to support the advanced features. uses about 5 megs of memory to run, but this may vary depending on your platform and configuration.

All required libraries as well as itself can be found on The home page.

You can find a list of changes at http://apps.kde.org/kdirstat.
